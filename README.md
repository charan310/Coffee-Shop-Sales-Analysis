# Coffee-Shop-Sales-Analysis

analyses conducted:

Sales Variation by Day of the Week: Analyzing which days experience higher or lower sales, helping to understand patterns and plan promotions or inventory accordingly.

Peak Times for Sales Activity: Identifying the hours or time periods when sales activity is at its highest, useful for staffing and operational efficiency.

Total Sales Revenue for Each Month: Calculating and comparing monthly revenue, which is essential for tracking business growth and forecasting future sales.

Sales Variation Across Store Locations: Understanding how sales differ between locations, which can highlight high-performing stores or areas needing improvement.
